## Posting Tips

1. **Best times:** Mon-Fri, 9am-4pm AEST
2. **Engage first:** Before posting, spend 10 min liking/commenting on BJJ accounts
3. **Stories:** Repost each feed post to Stories with a "New Post" sticker
4. **Reply to every comment** in the first hour — algorithm loves early engagement
5. **Cross-post:** Share to BJJ subreddits (r/bjj) and Facebook groups where appropriate
6. **Don't sell:** 80% of content is value/community, only 20% has direct CTAs
